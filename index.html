A Global Suicide Hotline Index

Suicidial.org is a global Suicide Prevention Hotline Index which was created under a concept by Rivindu Perera in November 2020.

Our key focus is on connecting all Suicide Prevention Hotlines in one Website in order to create quick response to any person with effects relating to suicide, without wasting time searching for various others.

We expect to completely broaden our coverage of Hotlines to 50 nations worldwide by 2025.
